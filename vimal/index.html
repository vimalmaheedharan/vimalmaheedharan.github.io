Who I Am

I'm Vimal Maheedharan, a Software Architect based in Kochi, India. Over the past 18+ years I've worked with startups, product companies and global enterprises, helping teams build scalable, resilient and secure software platforms.

My expertise spans cloud-native architecture, distributed systems, microservices, enterprise identity, DevOps, observability and engineering leadership. I enjoy designing systems that remain simple to maintain while handling enterprise-scale workloads.

I've contributed to projects across automotive, retail, media, fintech and e-commerce, working with technologies including AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes, Docker, Node.js, Java, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Kafka and Keycloak.

Beyond architecture, I'm passionate about mentoring engineers, simplifying complexity and creating technical strategies that align with long-term business goals.
